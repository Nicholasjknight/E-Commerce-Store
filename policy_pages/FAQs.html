Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

At Man Kave, we strive to make your shopping experience as seamless as possible. Below are answers to some of the most common questions our customers have. If you don’t see your question here, feel free to contact us!

Ordering

  • How do I place an order?

    Select your desired product, choose the quantity and options (if applicable), and click "Add to Cart." Proceed to checkout to complete your purchase.

  • Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

    Orders can be modified or canceled within 24 hours of placing them. Please contact us at support@mankave.store or call (813) 649-3341 as soon as possible.

Shipping

  • Where do you ship to?

    We ship to customers within the United States and internationally, excluding select regions. For details, please refer to our Shipping Policy.

  • How long does shipping take?

    Processing takes 1-2 business days. Delivery typically takes 2-10 business days, depending on your location.

  • How can I track my order?

    Once your order ships, you’ll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order using the "Track Order" link on our website.

Returns and Refunds

  • What is your return policy?

    We offer a 15-day return window for most products, in line with the AliExpress return policy. For details, visit our Return Policy page.

  • How do I initiate a return?

    To initiate a return, please contact our support team at support@mankave.store. Once approved, we’ll provide instructions on how to proceed.

Payments

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and Venmo for your convenience.

  • Is my payment information secure?

    Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to ensure your payment details are safe and secure.

Products

  • Are your products original and authentic?

    Yes, all our products are sourced from trusted suppliers to ensure quality and authenticity.

  • What should I do if an item is out of stock?

    If a product is out of stock, we recommend checking back later or contacting our support team for updates.

Support

  • How can I contact customer support?

    You can reach us via email at support@mankave.store, call us at (813) 649-3341, or use our Contact Us page.

  • What are your customer support hours?

    Our team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM EST.
